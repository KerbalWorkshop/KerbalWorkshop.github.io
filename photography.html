PHOTOGRAPHY

Since October 2024, I've been taking pictures of space through my backyard telescope. Below are the best photos I've taken. Prior to beginning astrophotography, I'd never realized there was so much beauty overhead that could be revealed by an amateur telescope sitting in a suburban backyard, and that discovery has been a welcome one. Taking pictures like these isn't easy — there's a laborious process that goes into creating each one — but at least I get to spend my time sitting under the stars.
